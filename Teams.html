Raider Team

Commander: Joe Walsh

The Emerson's Commando's Raider Team is a JROTC Physical Fitness Team. The Raiders engage in fitness competitions across the nation, specializing in events such as cold-weather terrain traversal, rucking, obstacle navigation, leadership-reaction training, and general cardiovascular fitness.    

Drill Team

Commander: Jake Kubitz

The Flannigan Rifles Drill Team is a JROTC team engaging in competitive military ceremony and armed exhibition events. The Drill Team are multi-time National Champions, hosting one of the greatest armed exhibition programs in the nation. The Drill Team is one of Marmion's oldest, and strongest groups.   

Rifle Team

Commander: Geno Shimkus

The Schroeder Sniper's Rifle Team is a JROTC Precision Marksmanship team. The Rifle team competes in traditional 10 meter 3 position air rifle competitions, culminating with the yearly National competition at the CMP Center, located in Camp Perry, Ohio.     

Robotics Team

Commander: Charlie McDaniel 

The Mission of the Robotics Team is to develop leadership, teamwork, communication and cooperation while increasing the cadets overall knowledge, expertise, and confidence in the interrelated disciplines of Science, Technology, and Math. The robotics team designs, builds, tests and improves VEX V5 robots with the goal of competing in a REC (Robotics Education Competition) Foundation event.

JLAB

The JROTC Leadership Symposium and Academic Bowl (JLAB) is a nationally recognized academic competition created exclusively for Army JROTC students and is sponsored by the US Army Cadet Command and the College Options Foundation.
