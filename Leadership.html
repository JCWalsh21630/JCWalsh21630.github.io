Battalion Commander Michael Greco

The Marmion Battalion operates under a cadet-run, military system of leadership. Each leadership role is operated by a duo of an officer and a non-comissioned officer. All officers and NCOs are directly in control of the Cadets in their element, and have a direct reporting line to the Cadet in the line of succession above him.
