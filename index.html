About

Marmion Academy is a catholic college-prep high school for young men. The Battalion has produced strong leaders under JROTC education since 1935.

The battalion hosts a variety of highly successful JROTC teams, including the repeated first-place Nationals Flannigan Rifles Drill Team, as well as the Emerson's Commando's Raider Team and the Schroeder-Sniper's Rifle Team.

The battalion functions as a student-run leadership structure, led by Cadet LTC. Michael Greco. 13 Cadets serve as officers and NCOs on the Staff executive board, planning, monitoring, and designating operations throughout the battalions.
