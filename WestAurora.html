Flannigan Rifles Takes 1st at West Aurora Drill Meet

Cadets took 1st in all events in the Armed Division

On January 28th, the Flannigan Rifles drill team competed in the Armed Division at Air Force JROTC West Aurora. The cadets took first overall in the armed division out of 12 schools, also earning first in every single event. The drill team looks to compete again on March 4th, hopefully repeating their first place achievements. Good luck to the drill cadets in the coming months!
