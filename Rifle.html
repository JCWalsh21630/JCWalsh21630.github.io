Marmion Rifle Team sends Cadet to CMP Nationals

Individual qualifier competes at Camp Perry CMP Center 

Rifle Team commander Cadet SGT. Geno Shimkus travelled to Camp Perry CMP Center for JROTC Rifle Nationals this week. The Marmion Rifle Team has had great success at national competitions in the past, with the Precision individuals taking 2nd and 3rd in the 2022 Nationals.
