Emerson's Commandos take 3rd in Calumet Competition  

Cadets beat out 10 other teams as the southern-most competitors   

On February 18th, the Marmion Emerson's Commandos Raider Team sent an 8-man contingent to the Annual Arctic Raider Challenge for the first time at Calumet National Guard Readiness Center. The cadets took 3rd overall out of 13 schools, competing as the southern-most team in the competition. Marmion was able to beat out a variety of legacy teams, including the home team of Calumet.
